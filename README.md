# Attainment Gaps at the University of Nottingham
## APP Analyst Interview Task — Data Analysis & Visualisation

---

## Overview

This project analyses degree attainment gaps across student characteristics 
at the University of Nottingham, benchmarked against sector averages and 
20 Russell Group peer providers. The analysis was produced for the 
University of Nottingham Access and Participation Plan (APP) team to 
guide intervention priorities for 2025-26 to 2028-29.
